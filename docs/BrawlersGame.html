The Brawlers Explained

Experience a thrilling adventure with The Brawlers! This 2D game, powered by Unity Game Engine, challenges you to survive or escape a challenging location. Play as a hero and put your skills to the test.

About the Team

Our team plans to create a unique 2D game that people will enjoy playing. Each of us has skills in software development, and we are putting in the effort to make this project a reality. We believe that our game will be different and interesting to players.
